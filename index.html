Save Space

Save Space is a photo-sharing platform designed to make collaboration simple and intuitive. Users can create albums (called spaces) and share them with others, who can then upload their own photos to the same album. The app uses magic link authentication for seamless access, with Supabase as the backend, AWS S3 for secure photo storage, and Next.js as the framework. Hosted on Vercel, Save Space is built for scalability and ease of use, making it effortless to share memories in a collaborative space.
